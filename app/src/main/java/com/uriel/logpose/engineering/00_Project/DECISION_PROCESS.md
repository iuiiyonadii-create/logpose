# DECISION_PROCESS

Version: 1.0

---

# Decision Levels

Level 1

Bug fixes.

Builder may decide.

---

Level 2

Internal implementation improvements.

Builder may decide.

Reviewer validates.

---

Level 3

New modules.

Architect defines.

Builder implements.

Reviewer validates.

---

Level 4

Architecture changes.

Architect approval required.

---

Level 5

Governance changes.

Project Owner + Architect approval required.

---

# Principle

Always choose the least invasive solution.
